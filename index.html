Who we are

An independent platform for digital development, education and cooperation.

ADP was established with the belief that digital transformation is not only a technological process, but also an educational, social, economic and cultural one.

Our activities focus on creating opportunities for individuals, institutions, researchers, educators and communities to benefit from technological advancement and to actively participate in the digital future.

We promote interdisciplinary cooperation and encourage the exchange of knowledge between academia, civil society, educational institutions, public bodies and the private sector.

Mission — Contribute to sustainable digital progress by supporting education, research, innovation, digital inclusion and international cooperation.

Vision — Become a recognized platform for digital innovation, academic cooperation, education and project development.

Innovation

We encourage creative thinking, technological development and new approaches to social and educational challenges.

Knowledge

Research, education and access to reliable information are essential foundations of progress.

Inclusion

We support equal access to digital opportunities and aim to reduce technological, educational and social inequalities.

Cooperation

Meaningful progress is achieved through partnerships between individuals, institutions, sectors and countries.

Responsibility

We promote the ethical, transparent and responsible use of technology and artificial intelligence.

Sustainability

We support solutions that create long-term educational, social, institutional and environmental value.

What we do

Six interconnected areas of activity.

From digital literacy in classrooms to AI ethics in research labs — every strand of our work reinforces the others.

Digital Transformation

Promoting the effective and responsible use of digital technologies in education, civil society, institutions and community development.

AI & Emerging Technologies

Following developments in artificial intelligence and encouraging its responsible use for education, research, innovation and social development.

Education & Digital Skills

Developing educational activities that improve digital literacy and lifelong learning for students, teachers, academics and communities.

Research & Academic Development

Supporting interdisciplinary research and academic cooperation in digital transformation, AI, education and social development.

Youth & Innovation

Encouraging young people to participate in technological, educational, scientific and entrepreneurial initiatives.

Digital Inclusion

Promoting equal access to knowledge, technology and digital opportunities, especially for underserved communities and rural areas.

Turning ideas into impact

Local, national and European project work.

ADP aims to initiate, develop, support and participate in projects at local, national, regional, European and international levels — including Erasmus+ and Horizon Europe.
